Our Mission

At CardioDetect, our goal is to revolutionize the early detection of cardiovascular diseases using AI-powered technology. We believe that timely symptom recognition can make the difference between life and death.

Our platform empowers users to input their symptoms and instantly receive intelligent feedback about the likelihood of a heart attack. This quick, accessible tool provides guidance and encourages users to seek medical attention when necessary.
